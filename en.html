About ECM Digital

We are a team of technology enthusiasts who believe that every business deserves digital transformation. We combine creativity with innovative technological solutions.

ECM Digital is a brand offering comprehensive and modern digital solutions. We specialize in web design, e-commerce, UX audits, and business process automation, helping companies develop in the digital world.

The ECM Digital brand operates within Scrum Software Sp. z o.o., which is the formal owner of all services we provide and the legally responsible entity. It is Scrum Software Sp. z o.o. that issues invoices, enters into contracts, and handles financial settlements.

Thanks to this solution, we combine modern branding with professional legal service, ensuring our clients full security and transparency of cooperation.
